Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Mango Bot

1. Overview

Mango Bot ("the Bot") is a private Discord bot. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

The Bot collects and stores the following data solely to provide its features:

  • Discord User IDs — to identify users with helper permissions and the bot owner.
  • Discord Server (Guild) IDs — to scope commands and configuration to the correct server.
  • Discord Role IDs — to determine whether a user has permission to use helper-only commands.
  • Auto-reply configurations — keyword triggers and response text set by authorised helpers.
  • Macro status data — version numbers, macro names, status values, and notes set by authorised helpers.
  • Channel and message IDs — to track the live status board message for automatic updates.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not read, log, or store the content of regular messages.
  • We do not collect email addresses, IP addresses, or any information outside of Discord.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

4. How Data Is Used

  • To respond to slash commands and deliver the correct output to the user.
  • To enforce permission levels (e.g. helper-only commands).
  • To persist auto-reply rules and macro statuses across bot restarts.
  • To automatically update the live macro status board message.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored in a private Supabase database. Access is restricted to the bot operator. Supabase's own privacy policy applies to the infrastructure layer.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as the Bot is active in a server. Server-specific data (roles, auto-replies, macro statuses) is removed upon request. There is no automated retention period.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data associated with your Discord User ID at any time by contacting the bot operator directly via Discord.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related requests, contact the bot operator on Discord.

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
